CEMMA EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE

CEMMA is guided by a committee of Faculty consisting of four from the Biological Science Department and four from the Materials Science Department. Each year, an associate director of the center is elected by the committee and the previous associate director becomes the director. The directorship alternates yearly between a materials scientist and a biological scientist. The center has two permanent staff members, John Curulli, Laboratory Director of Operations, and Alicia Thompson, Laboratory Director.

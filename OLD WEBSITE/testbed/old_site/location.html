CEMMA LOCATION

CEMMA is located in the former Human Centrifuge Building, originally used for NASA research in the 1950's and 1960's. This 5000 sq. ft. building with a solid, vibration free first floor is centrally located with respect to the science and engineering activities on campus. The large research instruments are on the ground floor of this building and the specimen preparation labs, photographic dark rooms, offices, and conference room are on the second floor. On the USC map we are located at D-6.

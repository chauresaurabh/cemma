September 2011 - THE CENTER ACQUIRES NEW TEM

The Vice President for Research, the Viterbi School of Engineering, and the Dornsife College of Letters, Arts and Sciences have teamed up to provide support for purchasing a transmission electron microscope (TEM). The TEM will be purchased from JEOL JEM-2100F and is an advanced 200kV Field Emission Electron Microscope featuring ultrahigh resolution.

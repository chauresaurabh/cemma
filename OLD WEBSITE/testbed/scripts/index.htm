CEMMA EXECUTIVE COMMITTEEe

    CEMMA is guided by a committee of Faculty consisting of four from the Life Science Department and four from the Physical Science Department. Each year, an associate director of the center is elected by the committee and the previous associate director becomes the director. The directorship alternates yearly between a physical scientist and a life scientist. The center has two permanent staff members, John Curulli, Director of Operations, and Alicia Thompson, Research Lab Specialist.
